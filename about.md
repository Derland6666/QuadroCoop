# QuadroCoop

Play Geometry Dash platformer levels with up to 4 players on one device!


## How to Play

QuadroCoop enables local multiplayer. Players can use separate keyboard keys or divide the screen into touch quadrants on mobile.


## Controls

### Keyboard (PC)

* **Player 1:** A (Left), W (Jump), D (Right)
* 
* **Player 2:** Left Arrow (Left), Up Arrow (Jump), Right Arrow (Right)
* 
* **Player 3:** J (Left), I (Jump), L (Right)
* 
* **Player 4:** F (Left), T (Jump), H (Right)
* 

### Touch (Mobile)

The screen is split into four quadrants:

* **Top-Left:** Player 1
* 
* **Top-Right:** Player 2
* 
* **Bottom-Left:** Player 3
* 
* **Bottom-Right:** Player 4
* 

## Settings
Open the Pause Menu while in a level and click the **QuadroCoop** button to toggle Player 3 and Player 4 on or off.




## Technical Features

* **Trigger Cloning:** Movement and Follow triggers are automatically synchronized across all players.
* 
* **Dual Portal Support:** P3 and P4 can independently trigger dual/single mode.
